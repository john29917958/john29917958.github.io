Someone says: Life is a process of constant choices. Have you ever fallen into a tough choice question? As a computer science postgraduate student, I regard myself a person that would make the world better through my professional skills of programming, but these days, something rushed into my life. A chance, a road totally diverged from the origin road I've been walked so far. How can I make a choice? Can my choice really lead me to my dream?
<!--more-->

## Review
### Before School
Before I entered elementary school, my father bought a computer with a jet aircraft game inside. I was really fascinated by this game. From then on, I decided to become a game engineer in the future because I want to share my happiness with all other people around the world.
Someday before I entered the elementary school, someday at the Christmas night, my father gave me a Christmas card with a Santa Clause on it. This Santa Clause was drawn by him. Since then, I started drawing and fell in love with it. I practiced a lot, I loved to create beautiful lines on the paper. Although I loved it, I still not changed my goal of becoming a game engineer.

### Elementary School
After I entered the elementary school, I started to create my own [LF2](http://lf2.net/) character. I somehow started my trip to a game engineer. I enjoyed the accomplishment of complete a brand new character with special animations and skills. At the same time, my drawing skill continuous to increase as well because I loved it, so I kept practicing it.

### Junior High School
Schoolworks increased a lot. Alghough my freetime was significantly compressed, I still kept doing what I loved. At this period of my life, I first get in touch with street dance.

### High School
I think high school is the toughest moment with heavy schoolworks. While I was studying in high school, I fell in love with street dance. As a B-Boy, my masters are uprock and footwork. At the same time, my drawing skill reached a different level. I stared to be aware of the body propertions and the structure of muscles of human beings. Unfortunately, I stopped making any game character. Until now, I have never written any line of code.

### University
Before I formally entered the university, I took game animation and game scene design courses. In these two courses, I learned how to create 3D game characters and apply animations to it. My dream was still very clear at this point.
Later, I started my university life. I started to learn how to program, how to code. At the same time, I joined the pop dance club in my university. However, the strange thing is, game development started to fade out from my life. After I started my university life, dance and programming occupied almost all of my life. As time went on, I reached junior grade in my university life. I hurt seriously in my junior grade while practicing a dance show. This year, was my toughest year in my life. Fortunately, I and some of my friends got a chance to be led by the mentors from Mozilla. I put all my negative emotions into the greatest motivation to the Mozilla project. As a result, I got the MVP of this project. And it is this time that my game engineer dream almost went away, disappeared completly. I started to feel that, as my point of view, web / internet technologies can provide more contribution to the world compared to game development. I can design and implement solutions to solve problems in the real world. After the Mozilla project, I started another project called [Service Desk](https://sd.cc.ncu.edu.tw/). This is a project that provides a platform for students to easily ask questions and find solutions about their life in NCU. After I finished this project, my game dream disappeared into hiding. I regard web engineer as my future work. I'm gonna use the web / internet technologies to bring human beings better life.
After I reached my final year of my university life, my dance skill improved to a great level.
Then... I became a postgraduate student.

### Graduate School
As soon as I started my life in graduate school, I got a mission to design the "Offline Recording" feature of the "KORAT" project in my lab. I put all my hard work into it, but rejected at the end. All my hard work was gone. This is the first time I experienced the feeling of gaining / accomplishing nothing after hard work.
The second mission I got was to implement a lot of new features of the project explorer of "KORAT" project within very short time. At the time I watched the structure of project explorer code, I thought the proper way is to refactor it into a good structure then can I start to extend it. So I did a lot of refactoring and asked professor if I can apply all these changes into it. At that time, I was a young shaver. Professor declined my refactoring. And yes, I think. I think he made the right decision. KORAT worked perfectly in the end, but the project explorer actually became a mess through my hands. At this time, my passion toward programming decreased a lot because a lot of my hard work gone all in a sudden.
Later, I got another mission which is to implement a real time web prototype for Smart Factory 4.0 demonstration within "10 days", and several modifications per day lasted about one month in total.
After all these experiences in my graduate life, I thought I kept doing things in a wrong way. I couldn't help but imagin that, outside school, whether crashing is more serious or not. I felt tired, I could hardly regain the enthusiasm to learn or sharpen my programming skills by myself. I'm futher apart from my trip to a web / internet engineer.

## Intruder
After all these experiences described above, I still regard myself as a web engineer in the future to make the world better.
In addition to my goal of becoming a web engineer, I also have two dreams now. One is to cure my father's diabetes, one is to help my mom buy a house she dreams. Strangely, a chance, a road totally diverged from the origin road I think I should go came out. This is the Grand Sun Enterprise, an enterprise that selling home SPA machine. I tested the machine myself and got increadible treatment effect. I think of my father at once. I have confidence that this machine can cure all my father's diseases. After I attended the camp held by Grand Sun, I found that, if I commit myself into this enterprise totally, it is possible for me to realize my mother's dream within very short period. Furthermore, the home SPA can help people, it can bring whole society advantages, it can solve a lot of people's problems of health. It matches my goal of making the world better.
But. But it is totally different from the origin me I regard as. I think I should become an engineer before I entered elementary school. I never diverged...until now. It is hard for me to abandon all I've learned, all I've walked, all I've experienced, all I've conquered, and then enter a domain that is totally new to me. Grand SPA rushed into my life, it strongly hit my head, it made me confused a lot of time. Even now, I cannot decide what kind of person I will be. Grand SPA can help me realize all my three goals, in very different domain. Just like... it's my destiney. Originally I think I'm destined to become a web / internet engineer to make this world better, but everything now seem like... I'm destined to enter the Grand SPA field. I want to help my father, my mother, and help people. All these dreams are well matched. It seems like I'm destined to change my web engineer trip. It seems like I'm destined to move myself into the Grand SPA industry.
My dear, how do you think? Do you believe in destiney?
