## Reason I Leave
These days, I kept wondering how strong I can be and how far I can go. I kept wondering how much value I can add to the world. At the age of 28, it's a very dangerous number. I missed a while, I worked hard twice to test my talents, abilities, skillsets, and potential. I had some tries to test if I can bring value to the world instead of company. Gradually, after these attempts and did some information collection, I got the theoretical hypothesis myself. I'm looking for skillsets. I'm looking for how much I can provide to the world. I see the "me" I can potentially be. But it's all hypothesis, I need experiment and hard work. I need SPEED. It's the reason I leave. I believe, you'll give me a bless, to unleash myself, to challenge, to fail, to struggle, to be stronger again and again. Goodbye TSMC, farewell, love and thank you all. Never forget, in the ABO team, once upon a time, there used to exist a person always says hard jokes, a person always with kind heart like a kid, a person with eternal enthusiasm, a person showed how powerful software design is. A fanatic of bringing tech. and art into Things, that's me, Tzu-Chao Wang.
